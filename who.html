We are a small group of Colombian citizens who want to share the way we see our country so that more people can enjoy and take care of the areas of nature reserves that we have. Our mission is to create spaces where people can belong through healthy, authentic, inclusive and sustainable travel; live with nature, perform activities a little daring and draw moments away from the world of technology, making this not mean sadness or stress, but instead be a break, an opportunity to connect in a deeper way with our environment. The spaces are and will always be in Colombia, we believe in the value of the country and with this, we think that we can offer more spontaneous, traditional moments, loaded with the culture of ours. We work so that you can enjoy activities in your free time a little different from the normal ones that you get used to. We invite you to try our services and know the beauty of Colombia.
